Decision logic tree

Priority-ordered checks in _evaluate_and_emit(). First match wins.

1
Position open and unrealised PnL < −max_loss_pct × entry cost
→ STOP_LOSS (CLOSE_ALL)
2
Position open and holding_days > max_hold_days
→ CLOSE_ALL (time exit)
3
P(Turbulent) > 0.80
→ CLOSE_ALL or HOLD (stay flat)
4
0.60 < P(Turbulent) ≤ 0.80
→ DELTA_HEDGE or HOLD (no entry)
5
Position open and IV spread < iv_exit_threshold
→ CLOSE_ALL (alpha gone)
6
Regime = Calm and IV spread > 2.0 vol pts and position FLAT
→ ENTER_LONG
7
No trigger
→ HOLD
Edge: ENTER_LONG only when (Regime == Calm) and (σ_model − σ_market > 2.0). CLOSE_ALL on turbulent regime, stop-loss, time exit, or spread compression.
